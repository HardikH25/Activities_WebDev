Recipe

Delicious Pancake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice (preferably leftover, cold rice works best)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp oil (vegetable or sunflower)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • ½ cup mixed veggies (peas, carrot, corn - optional)
  • 1-2 tbsp soy sauce
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Spring onion (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan or wok.
  2. Add the chopped onion and stir-fry until soft.
  3. Add the veggies and cook for 2–3 minutes.
  4. Push veggies to the side of the pan. Crack in the eggs and scramble them.
  5. Add the cooked rice and mix everything well.
  6. Pour in soy sauce, add salt & pepper. Stir-fry for another 2 minutes.
  7. Garnish with spring onion and serve hot.
